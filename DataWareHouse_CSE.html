Data-Warehousing-Mining

A Data Warehouse refers to a place where data can be stored for useful mining. It is like a quick computer system with exceptionally huge data storage capacity. Data from the various organization's systems are copied to the Warehouse, where it can be fetched and conformed to delete errors. Here, advanced requests can be made against the warehouse storage of data. Data mining refers to the analysis of data. It is the computer-supported process of analyzing huge sets of data that have either been compiled by computer systems or have been downloaded into the computer.
